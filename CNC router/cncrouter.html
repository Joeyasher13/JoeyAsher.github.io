CNC Router

This project was a fun one, in it I built a CNC router with a workable volume of 46" x 46" x 4". Although most of the hardware for this project was bought from a supplier in China, I was able to put together all of the electronics. To get a better understanding of whats under the hood of a CNC machine, I usedLinuxCNC as the control software. I put a lot of time into organizing all of the electronics into a control box, and although it doesn't look like a traditional controls box, it does a good job oraganizing all of the electronic components.
