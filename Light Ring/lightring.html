LED Light Ring

I took this project as an oppertunity to show off my design skills by createing a functional, and beautiful, LED light ring. The actual light ring PCB was originally designed to fit inside an underwater ROV, for a senior design project I worked on. I have recently been incorperating more graphics in my designs, and am in the process of buying a die cutting machine to ease in the creation of these graphics.

Stay tuned for updates!

This project is still underdevelopment as of . Right now I'm am waiting on the circuit boards from the manufacturer, and in the mean time I have been preparing the plastic housing.
